Aeropress coffee

Return to recipes

Description

An Aeropress is an affordable, easy method of making good-tasting coffee.

Ingredients

  • 15g freshly ground coffee
  • 250ml water, straight off the boil

Steps

  1. Insert the plunger into the Aeropress body and invert it, so the body opening is facing the sky
  2. Add the freshly ground coffee
  3. Add the boiled water and stir
  4. Start a timer for 4 minutes
  5. If using a paper filter, moisten it
  6. Attach filter and cap to the Aeropress
  7. Optionally, pre-heat a robust cup or mug (using hot water from the tap is fine)
  8. When the 4 minutes have elapsed, carefully upend the Aeropress on your cup or mug
  9. Push plunger down slowly until all of the water has been pushed through and you hear a hiss of air
